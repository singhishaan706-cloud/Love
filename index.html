01
📍 The Move

A new city, a new life

Main Kolkata se Delhi shift hua. Naya sheher, naye log, naye sapne. Maine apni first coaching join ki — bina jaane ki ye jagah meri zindagi ka sabse khoobsurat mod banne wali hai.

02
✨ The First Sight

Not love at first sight

Wahin maine usse pehli baar dekha. Honestly? It wasn't love at first sight. Hum 1 - 1.5 saal saath padhe, normal classmates ki tarah. Mujhe pata bhi nahi tha ki ye ladki dheere dheere meri zindagi mein kitni important hone wali hai.

"Sometimes the best stories start quietly."

03
💭 The Realisation

When she left, I felt it

Phir ek din wo tution chhod ke chali gayi. Kuch mahine baad mujhe ehsaas hua — main usse miss kar raha hoon. Bahut zyada. Tab samjha — yaar, ye toh love hai. Aur tab tak bahut der ho chuki thi… kyunki mere paas usse contact karne ka koi zariya nahi tha.

04
📱 The Hunt

Snapchat detective mode 🔍

Maine Snapchat install kiya. Tution group se uska number nikala, save kiya, contacts ko Snap se link kiya — aur finally uska Snap mila. Kuch din baad Insta bhi. Lagta tha jaise kuch jeet liya ho. Par bas wahin tak.

05
🎂 Birthday #1

She came. Goosebumps.

Mera birthday aaya. Maine usse invite kiya. Itne mahino baad jab usse dekha — mere rongte khade ho gaye. I was literally amazed. Hum bahut enjoy kiye… par wo 1 ghanta bhi sirf 10 minute jaisa laga. Time uske saath hamesha bhaagta hai.

06
🎂 Birthday #2

The fear of losing a friend

Agle saal phir maine usse invite kiya. Iss baar dil mein tha — aaj bata dunga. Par darr lag raha tha — kahin dosti na toot jaaye. Maine Ishika (humari mutual friend) se pucha. Usne kaha — "na batana behtar hai." Maine maan li. Wo aayi, hum phir enjoy kiye, aur phir wahi — 1 ghanta = 10 minute.

07
💗 4th January 2026 • 11:58 PM

The night I finally said it

New Year mein ghoomne nahi mila tha. Ghar pe baithe baithe maine ek "ask me anything" story daali. Aur ussi ne pucha — "who is your crush?"

Main baat ghuma sakta tha. Bachne ke 100 raaste the. Par maine socha — kab tak?

Aur 11:58 PM par maine bata diya. Uska reaction tha — "koi nahi." Maine socha — isse achha toh "no" bol deti. Darr laga ki block na kar de. Par next day usne story daali aur khud puchne lagi ki main kaisi lag rahi hoon. Main aur confuse ho gaya 😭

08
💄 The Lip Balm Story

Mixed signals & mini missions

Ab silsila chal raha hai. Main thoda flirt karta hoon, wo kehti hai — "mere liye itne efforts mat daal." Par main kya karoon, dil maanta hi nahi.

Ek din usne lip balm ki reel bheji. Flow mein maine bol diya "done." Paise the nahi. Bahen se Raksha Bandhan wale gift ke badle ₹150 le liye. Chhup chhup ke lip balm dene gaya — soch ke ki isi bahane mil bhi lunga. Par usne apna chhota bhai bhej diya 😅 Bura laga… par phir socha — at least uss tak pohoch toh gaya.

"Chhoti chhoti baatein, jo sirf main yaad rakhta hoon."

And so…

Yeh meri kahaani hai

Tumhe pata hai? Tumse milne se pehle main itne dil se kabhi kisi ke baare mein nahi socha tha. Tum coaching ki ek normal ladki nahi ho — tum meri sabse khoobsurat memory ho.

Main nahi jaanta yeh kahaani kahan jaayegi. Par itna jaanta hoon — yeh likhne layak thi. Aur tum janne layak ho.
